Our Heritage

Founded in the heart of a city known for its vibrant heritage and bustling trade routes, our restaurant is a tribute to the timeless fusion of tradition and modern craft. Inspired by the resilience and hospitality that define our history, we began as a small project dedicated to reviving the "Baithak" culture—a place where ideas are shared over a perfect brew. From the aromatic influence of ancient spice trails to the contemporary "Morning Scrim" of today’s fast-paced world, our menu reflects a journey through time. Every cup of our "Executive" Espresso and every "Networking" Latte is crafted with the same dedication to quality that has made our culinary culture famous for generations. We aren't just serving coffee; we are preserving a legacy of connection, one sip at a time.
